# PHASE_35_BRIEF

## Queue

Result sharing competition loop.

## Goal

Let players share a completed score result as a light social challenge while keeping rewards and ranked submission fair.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Result panel includes a share action after completion.
- Share service tracks `result_share`.
- Share success does not grant coins, recipe pieces, score, or rank advantage.
- SFX includes a lightweight result-share confirmation.
- Unit and mobile browser tests cover the flow.

## Commander Notes

This supports competitive spread without creating review risk from reward-linked sharing.
