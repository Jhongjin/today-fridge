# PHASE_13_BRIEF

## Queue

Personal best competition loop.

## Goal

Add a simple replay driver that makes the first daily board feel competitive before social leaderboard integration is ready.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The play screen shows the saved personal best for today's board.
- The play screen shows the gap to the current best while the player is below it.
- A completed round records a higher personal best locally.
- The result panel shows the improvement delta.
- Unit and mobile browser tests cover the storage and UI path.

## Commander Notes

This is intentionally local-first. It supports addiction and competition without introducing public-rank pressure or reward-policy risk.
