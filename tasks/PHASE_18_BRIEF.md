# PHASE_18_BRIEF

## Queue

Recipe-piece progress display.

## Goal

Make recipe pieces feel meaningful immediately after the player claims a completion reward.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Result screen shows active recipe progress.
- Progress starts at `0/3`.
- Claiming the completion reward advances progress to `1/3`.
- Mobile browser tests cover the progress update.

## Commander Notes

This is the smallest useful recipe-book step before adding a full collection screen.
