# PHASE_14_BRIEF

## Queue

Non-blocking first-play tutorial hints.

## Goal

Reduce first-board confusion by surfacing the safest opening route directly on the playable board.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The first screen shows a short hint rail.
- The first safe match route is highlighted.
- After the match clear, the recipe route is highlighted.
- The hint rail disappears after completion or manual close.
- Mobile browser tests cover the hint progression.

## Commander Notes

This is intentionally not a modal tutorial. The player can keep tapping, ignore it, or close it.
