# PHASE_36_BRIEF

## Queue

Local daily streak habit cue.

## Goal

Show a simple consecutive-day count on the first screen to reinforce the daily fridge loop without adding payout pressure.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Local streak storage starts at 1 day.
- Same daily seed does not increment twice.
- Adjacent daily seeds continue the streak.
- Missed dates reset the streak.
- First screen shows the streak beside the refresh cue.

## Commander Notes

The streak is informational only. It does not grant coins, recipe pieces, rank, or Toss points.
