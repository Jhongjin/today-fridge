# RECIPES

## 1. Recipe Role

Recipes are not long cooking instructions. They are lightweight collection goals and high-value clears.

MVP recipe cards should show:

- Dish name.
- Required ingredients.
- Difficulty: easy, normal, tricky.
- Score value.
- Short positive caption.

## 2. Recipe Clear Rule

When the prep tray contains all required ingredients for an active recipe, those ingredients clear and the recipe completes.

MVP rules:

- Recipe requirements use 3 ingredients by default.
- Some advanced recipes use 4 ingredients.
- A recipe can share ingredients with another recipe, creating route choices.
- The first board should expose only one active recipe.

## 4. MVP Recipe Tiers

### Easy

- Three ingredients.
- No shared conflict.
- Strong household familiarity.
- Used in tutorial and first daily board.

### Normal

- Three ingredients.
- Some shared ingredients.
- Better score.

### Tricky

- Four ingredients or shared goals.
- Introduced after the first prototype.

## 5. Active Recipe Rotation

Daily board should expose:

- One main recipe.
- One optional side recipe from Tier 3 onward.

The recipe book may show locked recipes, but the play screen should not show too many recipes at once.
